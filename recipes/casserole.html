Description

casserole with peacan topping, very good for thanksgiving

Ingredients

  • sweet potatos
  • eggs
  • white sugar
  • milk
  • vanilla extract
  • salt

Recipe Steps

  1. cook and mash the sweet potatos
  2. mix Ingredients
  3. sprinkle topping on the sweet potato
  4. bake until light brown
